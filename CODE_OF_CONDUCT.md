# Code of Conduct

We believe in the **KISS** principle (**Keep It Simple, Stupid**), and that applies to our community guidelines as well. 

Instead of a long, complicated document, here are our simple rules for interacting in the FrankUI community:

1. **Be Respectful:** Treat others with kindness and respect. No harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
2. **Keep it Simple & Constructive:** Focus on solving problems, reporting bugs, and suggesting features. Keep discussions clear, helpful, and on-topic.
3. **No Spam:** Don't advertise unrelated services, spam issues, or post low-effort/off-topic messages.

### Enforcement
If you behave like a decent human being, everything will be fine. If you violate these simple rules repeatedly or act maliciously, you will be blocked from this repository.

Keep it simple, keep it friendly!
